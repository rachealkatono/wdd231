Rafael Rafting - Adventure Awaits

Site Name

Rafael Rafting

The name represents an adventurous whitewater rafting experience, emphasizing excitement and exploration.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an informational and booking platform for adventure enthusiasts seeking thrilling whitewater rafting experiences. It will provide details about different rafting trips, safety guidelines, pricing, and contact options for bookings.

Planned Site Content

  • Home Page: Overview of Rafael Rafting, its mission, and what makes it unique.
  • Trips Page: Detailed descriptions of available rafting trips, including locations, levels, detailed pricing, and what to expect.
  • Gallery Page: A collection of images showcasing past rafting adventures, the excitement, and the rafting experiences.
  • Contact Us Page: A user-friendly form for inquiries about trips, bookings, or general information, with additional contact details.

Scenarios

  • What are the safety measures in place for rafting trips?
  • How do I book a whitewater rafting adventure?

Color Schema

  • Deep Blue (#004080) - Used for headings and navigation.
  • Light Aqua (#B3E5FC) - Used for backgrounds and accents.

Typography

  • Poppins - Used for headings.
  • Lato - Used for body text.
